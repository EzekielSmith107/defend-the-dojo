Defend the Dojo

Since the reign of the Mountain King, the land has been peaceful for centuries. But now, everything has changed. With strange omens and natural disasters happening on the daily, the people of the village Acorn are uneasy. With a long tradition of training some of the best ninjas in the area, villagers prepare for the worst. Now, a local medicine man reports seeing dangerous foes approaching while he was out gathering shrooms.
Prepare your defenses, for the village must not fall into the hands of the enemy.

Controls

Place ninjas using coins by clicking on a light colored tile.

Defeat enemies to earn more coins.

You lose hearts if an enemy reaches the town.

If your hearts reach zero, Game Over.
